Parent Teachers Association

At HCA we strongly believe in a community school. The school belongs to all shareholders who support it. We are open to work with the parents through a Parents Represented Board elected by the parents themselves. The business community that abuzz the community around and that form HCA’s business partners are welcome to contribute to the wellbeing of the school. The religious civic communities and government are all welcome to contribute in well being of HCA.
