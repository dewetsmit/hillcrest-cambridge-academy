At HCA we believe that sports and games encompass more than just the benefit of physical activity.



In actual essence sport increases self-esteem and mental alertness. It is therefore imperative that all school going children must have access to sporting and games.


Sport does not only empower children and promote higher self-esteem, but it also motivates children, enables them to earn higher grades as a healthy body is receptive and conducive to meaningful and productive learning.


Benefits of Sports

There are numerous benefits in sports which include maintaining a healthy weight, preventing chronic diseases, and acquiring skills necessary to maintain a healthy lifestyle even after their schooling is over. This is also fundamentally important when we acknowledge the fact that not all learners are academicians. Others may pursue sport as a career path and end up becoming sporting professionals and earn a living through it.
