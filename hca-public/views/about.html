Hillcrest Cambridge Academy

Hillcrest Cambridge (Academy HCA) was founded in 2017 to provide quality and affordable education to the Randburg and surrounding communities. As South Africa is a Rainbow Nation, the institution is therefore an all-encompassing serving any nationality, colour, race creed, and multi-pluralism. In this context a Cambridge Curriculum with some aspects of the CAPS Curriculum attributes will be a dream come true for what most parents have been looking for without success. Over and above our institution is based on Christian ethos and principles whilst acknowledging and encompassing religion pluralism.

A Cambridge curriculum is a passport to any university as it is internationally recognized without undergoing some exemption procedures. For local South African students the Cambridge curriculum brings maturity and a university approach which is an enormous benefit when learners progress to tertiary education.

HCA is what a school should be. It is a complete package and an all season’s provider of the child’s and parent’s needs in the field of education and pursuit for the future. The only valuable inheritance that a parent can give to a child is Quality Education. You can count on us, just support us, and your child will achieve goals, dreams, ambitions and aspirations. With HCA your child is set to “Sour To Excellence”.
