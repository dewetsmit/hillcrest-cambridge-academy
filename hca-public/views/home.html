Welcome to Hillcrest Cambridge Academy

A Cambridge curriculum is a passport to any university as it is internationally recognized without undergoing some exemption procedures. For local South African students the Cambridge curriculum brings maturity and a university approach which is an enormous benefit when learners progress to tertiary education.


HCA is what a school should be. It is a complete package and an all season’s provider of the child’s and parent’s needs in the field of education and pursuit for the future. The only valuable inheritance that a parent can give to a child is Quality Education. You can count on us, just support us, and your child will achieve goals, dreams, ambitions and aspirations.


With HCA your child is set to “Soar To Excellence”.
